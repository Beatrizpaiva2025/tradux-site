Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my certified translation?

+

Our standard delivery time for certified translations is 24 hours. We also offer express options: 12-hour delivery (50% additional cost) and 4-hour delivery (100% additional cost) for urgent needs.

Are your certified translations accepted by USCIS?

+

Yes! All our certified translations comply with USCIS requirements and include the necessary certification statement. Our translations are accepted by USCIS, universities, and other government agencies.

What file formats do you accept?

+

We accept a wide range of file formats including PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG, and TXT. If you have a different format, please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

How does your AI + human review process work?

+

Our process combines the speed of AI with the accuracy of human expertise. First, your document is processed by our advanced AI translation engine, which has been trained on millions of professional translations. Then, a human translator reviews the result, ensuring terminology accuracy, context appropriateness, and proper formatting.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

+

Absolutely! We stand behind our translations with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If your translation is not accepted by the intended recipient due to quality issues, we'll provide a full refund or revise the translation at no additional cost.
